Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 15, 2025

Currigo does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

This app uses the public Frankfurter API to fetch exchange rate data. No personally identifiable information (PII) is ever transmitted or stored.

The app does not use cookies, analytics, or advertising services.

Internet access is used solely to retrieve currency exchange rates in real-time. No data is sent to any server owned or controlled by the developer.

If you have questions about this policy, feel free to contact the developer via the Play Store listing.
